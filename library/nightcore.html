Nightcore

By Clive Python | 14jammar

I'd just like to remind people that the OwlMan Library's motto is "Good to Forget", so keep that in mind while reading this.

Some time in early 2014 I was invited to a Skype group channel, a lot of the people in said channel were fans of Anime, I'll admit that I've never really been into Anime, the only time that I've watched any was on Chrismas just because it was on the telly, anyway, I saw that the creator of the group had posted a link, a link to a video called "Hanging Tree".

Good God, when I played the music it was just.... bad, it was like Alvin and The Chipmunks, but Anime.

Looking up Nightcore on YouTube you will - for some reason - you will find 14,800,000 results, most of them are just covers of much better songs.

It's crazy what covers you can find; David Bowie, AC/DC, Pink Floyd and even They Might Be Giants. Is nothing sacred to these people?

If I was you, I would stay far, far, far away from nightcore, it's a downward spiral.

I'm sorry if this has been a crap read, but I do this for free, so shut it!
