Wedding After The Battle of Britain

Last night I was surfing Reddit when I came across /r/Jessicamshannon, a subreddit that wants the reader to "imagine that the victim is you or your family. Imagine feeling what they feel. I want all of my albums to help make history come alive". When looking at the subreddit, I found a post called "Wedding after the battle of Britain"; it's quite weird seeing a seemingly normal thing in an abnormal environment. I think these photos speak for themselves.
