Prepare to Meet Thy God

Therefore thus will I do unto thee, O Israel: and because I will do this unto thee, prepare to meet thy God, O Israel.
- Amos 4:12 (KJB)

Today I wish to share a photo that I found online some time ago about The Trubbles taken c. 1970 in Belfast. The photo shows a British Ammunition technical officer (ATO) taking a long walk to a suspect device, located at the junction of Manor Street and Oldpark Road.

In totial 23 ATOs were killed from 1968 to 1998.

"The Long Walk" is a Public Domain work by Snozzer.
