The End of the Network Neighborhood

By Clive Python | 14jammar

Hey there! Do you have permission to use our banner on your site? Last time we checked, you aren't an official member of the Network Neighborhood. If you want to join, please send one of our members an e-mail through our webzone, otherwise please remove the banner in question. Thanks! ~The Network Neighborhood Team

- The Network Neighborhood talking to gates

The 5th of July 2016 was the end of the Network Neighborhood, a Neocities webring that less than 1% of the site was apart of. I would say that I had no idea that it was ending, but I knew.

Lots of its members were leaving the NN like mad, 98plus even said (on his blog) "the webring i'm in and co-founded (remember that?) is on the verge of shutting down. yeah. the best way we can stay alive if is we get more members." Well, they only got one new member.

What's even more, on the NN YouTube channel 98plus posted a video (that's now been removed [archive]) saying the same thing he said on his blog, he also told us that "We need new members as soon as possable" and that we should apply to join the NN. I did apply on the day the video was put on YouTube, the fourth of April, four months later and still no reply from them. Jeez.

It even got to the point that I went to their Discord server (that is no longer up) and asked why they had not contacted me, this is what they said (two things to note; one I was 17 at the time and two, there was a NN member who was 17 (or even 16) at the time):

17th of June 2016

Marin the Technomancer: yeah, we have a general age requirements just because many of our members are over 18 and it's just... there can be issues i'd like to adoid.

OwlMan: Am I to young then?

Marin the Technomancer: I don't think so, have you applied @OwlMan

OwlMan: I did but the NN never got back

Marin the Technomancer: Oh. That might have been during my absence, i'll check the applications.

OwlMan: Okay :D

They never got back to me.
