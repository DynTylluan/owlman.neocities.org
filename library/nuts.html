Nuts

By Clive Python | 14jammar

Nuts I, II & III are some of the best Doom WADs, do you want your computer to goto 2 FPS? Want to be killed by 1000+ monsters? If you said yes to both... then get help. Nuts is what it says on the tin, the game is crazy. The only Nuts game that will play okay on my computer is Nuts II and even then it is still slow.

Nuts III is where it just gets silly with over Over 12,00 monsters. Good luck not using God Mode!

What is weird is the story for Nuts:

You are assigned to destroy the final Hellspawn army on Litrivin IV. The government sends you all alone becuase you got drunk at the General's Birthday party last year and he never liked you since. Since this is the biggest Hellspawn army ever esstablished, the cheap-ass General sends you with nothing but a pea shooter. Good luck.
