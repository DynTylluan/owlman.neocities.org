Bimbo's Initiation

By Clive Python | 14jammar

Made in 1931 by Fleischer Studios, Bimbo's Initiation is damn right bizzar, if I had no imagination I would say something on the lines of "Woah man, this carton is so weird, they must of been on, like, weed or shrooms when they made this!"

To quote TV Tropes;

Any work whose creation seems to have involved large amounts of hallucinogens, cocaine, crack, or any other illicit substance that makes people think really weird ideas are also really good ones. The plot hinges on bizarre transformations, freakish-looking creatures, and nonsensical actions that only seem to make sense in realms of logic far removed from your own. That it was the product of a deranged mind looks like a foregone conclusion.

And then you find out that it most certainly wasn't.

It's Public Domain, so try and sue me!

Or watch a copy of it on YouTube

At the start you would have seen a certain mouse, well this was a lawyer-friendly cameo from Mickey Mouse.

Also; Back in the 1930s, "Bimbo" was slang for "tough guy".
