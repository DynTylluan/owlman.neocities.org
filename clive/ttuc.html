PIF

Short for "Public Information Film", a PIF is a film made in the UK to teach and inform the public about a possible danger.

Some examples of this would be "Protect and Survive", "King of the Road", "Apaches" etc.

PIFs are not to be confused with propaganda. PIFs and propaganda are two different things.

While the term "PIF" refers to films made in the UK they are sometimes used to talk about none-British films.

Link Rot

Link rot is a term used to describe any link that now leads to a 404 page, this can be for a number of reasons;

  • The website was removed or is dead
  • The owner of the site removed the page
  • Links may be removed as a result of legal action or court order
  • A website might change its domain name. Links pointing to the old name might then become invalid.
  • More Causes
