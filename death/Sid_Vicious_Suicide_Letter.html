The transcript of John Simon Ritchie A.K.A. Sid Vicious suicide letter
10th of May 1957 – 2nd of February 1979
He was 21 at the time of his death

John Beverly, born John Simon Ritchie, later named Sid Vicious was an English musician, most known
for being the bass guitarist for the punk rock band the Sex Pistols and notorious for his arrest for the 
alleged murder of his girlfriend, Nancy Spungen.
