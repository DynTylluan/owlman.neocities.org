OWLMAN
In a letter to his girlfriend, Barbara John George Haigh wrote
24th of July 1909 – 10th of August 1949
He was 40 at the time of his death 
Executed by hanging

**************************************************************************************************************


It is difficult to say farewell under these circumstances, but you will understand that you will always be in my thoughts. 
You know I have been proud of our association: it has always been an honourable one. 
I shall remember your great kindness and devotion. 
Now I must leave you.
